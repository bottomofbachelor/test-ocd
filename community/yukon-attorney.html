Expungements, Appeals, and Parole Violations

Many people talk of clearing their record or sealing their record. This process is referred to as expungement (or in some cases, expunction) of the criminal record. In Oklahoma, there are two types of record expungement for which an individual may qualify. The first, defined under 22 O.S. 991c of the state code, allows for the expungement of a record following successful completion of a deferred sentence.

We Proudly Serve Yukon Oklahoma


Yukon Oklahoma is a western suburb of Oklahoma City.  It located in eastern Canadian county.  The city has a long history dating back to the 1890s. It was named after the area of Canada that experienced a gold rush in the late 19th century.

Now the town is a popular place to live for those who work in Oklahoma City.  It is just minutes away from OKC by taking Interstate 40 east.  It is an area where we often get to practice as criminal defense attorney Yukon OK.

Yukon is a great place to live for those commuting to OKC as there are several large interstates close by.
