Expungements, Appeals, and Parole Violations

Expungement, or expunction, is the process of "clearing" or sealing a criminal record.

If you completed a deferred sentence or served your time for a crime, you have paid your dues. A conviction on your record can keep you from moving on. If you're ready to leave the past behind you, filing a petition for expungement of your record can give you a fresh start. Our defense attorneys can give you the fresh start you need.  Call today for a free consultation.
