Expungements, Appeals, and Parole Violations

Many people talk of clearing their record or sealing their record. This process is referred to as expungement (or in some cases, expunction) of the criminal record. In Oklahoma, there are two types of record expungement for which an individual may qualify. The first, defined under 22 O.S. § 991c of the state code, allows for the expungement of a record following successful completion of a deferred sentence.

Serving All of Bethany Oklahoma

Bethany is a fast growing suburb of Oklahoma City.  Many of the area's residents commute to OKC for work to avoid the higher cost of housing and the crowds of the city.  While the town has been around since 1895 it wasn't until more recently that the population started to grow.  As more and more people found work in the downtown area, they needed a safe place to raise their families that allowed easy access to their jobs.

Soon a strong retail economy sprung up with all of the supporting infrastructure.  This created more jobs and led to more people moving into the area.  Oklahoma is still a religious state and so along with retail and housing came churches.  The Bethany First Church Nazarene and the  are just two of several in the area.
