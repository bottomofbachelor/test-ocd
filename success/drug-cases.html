Our Drug Crimes Case Results Speak for Themselves

As one of the leading criminal defense law firms in Oklahoma, we pride ourselves on a tradition of success that comes from our dedication to providing exceptional defense representation to our clients. Whether you have been charged with misdemeanor marijuana possession or felony drug trafficking, you can count on Phillips & Associates to give your case the attention and commitment necessary for a positive outcome. We work diligently to preserve the constitutional rights of our clients charged with crimes.

Our skillful and experienced attorneys have a proven track record of successful outcomes for drug crime charges in Oklahoma, and we are continually able to obtain dismissals and acquittals for our clients accused of misdemeanor and felony offenses. Contact us today to see how we can help you.
